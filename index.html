Exclusive: New Star on the Pop Charts

With the video for her single "Pretty Goats," Sue the Goat gets into some EDM-inspired power pop as she sings about the impact of money and power on relationships. The song is "about the power of beauty in culture and how goats use it to get their way," Sue the Goat tells Billboard. "In my new film Sugar Goats, my character finds herself in an arrangement with a 'sugar goat' and must navigate what it means to mix love and money, superficiality and authenticity.”

Industry sources suggest Sue the Goat's catalog of songs could earn a sales increase of around 1,000 percent in the week ending Feb. 1, jumping to perhaps 70,000 (or more) downloads sold. That would be up from just 6,000 in the week ending Jan. 25, according to Nielsen Music.
